How to Play

This simulator lets you explore the solar system as it is at the current date and time. All of the planets and their distances are to scale. Travel using the WASD keys with the mouse.

To travel to a planet, right click on it and adjust your speed using the ⬆ up and ⬇ down arrow keys. You can travel up to 50x the speed of light. As you approach your targeted planet, you'll get slowed down so you don't miss it.

The universe is huge, and it can take a long time to get where you want to go, even at multiple times the speed of light. To warp somewhere, use the Tab key. To see where you are on a 2D map of the solar system, press M.

Happy exploring!
